Digital Onboarding

Open a saving account online 24/7 without visiting a branch! With full biometric features, the process becomes faster, easier, and more secure. Customers only need to take a photo of their ID card, take a selfie with the ID, complete live detection, and fill in their personal data.

Time Deposit

Customers can open a time deposit online by selecting the tenor and maturity option, with the flexibility to modify maturity option or withdraw the deposit before maturity (break), subject to applicable terms and conditions.

Debit Card Management

Debit Card Management is a feature that allows customers to conveniently manage and control their debit cards. Designed for ease and flexibility, this feature provides customers with full control over their debit cards.

Cash In Cash Out

Customers can conveniently deposit and withdraw cash at all Indomaret stores. Simply generate a token through the app and present it to the Indomaret cashier to complete the transaction quickly and easily.

Cardless Cash Withdrawal

Customers can easily withdraw cash at any ATM LINK. Simply generate a token through the app, then enter the token and phone number at the ATM LINK to complete the transaction quickly and conveniently.
